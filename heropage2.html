Delicious Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the top and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
